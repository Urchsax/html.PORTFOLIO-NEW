TOP 4 MOVIES IN NIGERIA IN 2024

List of Top Trending Nigerian Series on Netflix

With the dawn of a new year, Nollywood is set to unravel a cinematic chain
that promises to captivate, inspire, and etch lasting memories.
Join us as we run through five of the highly anticipated releases of 2024.

1. 77: THE FESTAC CONSPIRACY

Following the massive success of his 2016 political and historical thriller,
’76’, Izu Ojukwu is pulling his gang together for yet another thriller
based on real events. His gang is our favourite duo: Ramsey Nouah and Rita Dominic.
This movie also serves as a sequel to the 2016 movie, ’76, and if we’re setting expectations
based on that, you can be sure our expectations are very high for this movie.
Other notable cast we’re excited to see include Ibinabo Fibresima, Seun Kuti, Daniel K. Daniel, and many more!

2. ANIKULAPO: RISE OF THE SPECTRE

Famed film producer and director Kunle Afolayan had everyone talking in 2022 with the
release of his critically acclaimed ‘Anikulapo,’ which left us wanting more. Now, he’s set
to release a four-part series of the same title, with key players Kunle Remi, Bimbo Ademoye, and Sola Sobowale reprising their roles.

3. OLOTURE: THE SERIES

Among the movies being serialised is Ebony Life’s ‘Oloture,’ which was first released as a
feature-length in 2019. It is set to premiere on Netflix sometime this year. Sharon Ooja,
Beverly Osu, and Omoni Oboli will retain their roles, while actors Bucci Franklin and
Stan Nze will join the ensemble.

4. TO KILL A MONKEY

One of Nollywood’s finest directors, Kemi Adetiba, is set to release another crime thriller
this year. To Kill a Monkey will premiere on Netflix and stars the likes of William
Benson, Bucci Franklin, Chidi Mokeme, Damilola Adegbite, Ireti Doyle, and many more.
